üíé Token Economy

IAM operates on a deflationary economic model where tokens are permanently removed from circulation through strategic mechanisms, creating sustained value appreciation for all participants.

üî• Token Burning Mechanisms

In every major activity within the ecosystem, a portion of tokens is permanently burned (removed from circulation). This creates a continuous reduction in total supply, leading to increased scarcity and value appreciation.

  • üõí Token Purchase: During buy transactions, a backing fee is applied and tokens are burned
  • üí∞ Token Sale: During sell transactions, additional tokens are burned to maintain ecosystem balance
  • üìù Paid Registration: A portion of registration fees contributes to token burning
  • üéØ Reward Claims: During reward claiming, tokens are burned as part of the tax mechanism
  • üõçÔ∏è Product Purchases: When purchasing from vendors, tokens are burned as part of the transaction

üìà Deflationary Impact

Every transaction removes tokens from circulation. This continuous reduction in total supply creates a deflationary spiral where:

  • Scarcity increases with every transaction
  • Token value appreciates due to reduced supply
  • Upward price pressure is maintained continuously
  • Ecosystem balance is preserved through controlled deflation

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy IAM tokens?
Use the buyTokens() function with DAI. Minimum 1 DAI required. Dynamic buy cap based on contract balance.
How do I sell IAM tokens?
Use the sellTokens() function. Minimum 1 IAM required. No sell cap restriction.
What are the fees?
0% developer fee + dynamic backing fee (1-2% based on contract balance). Fees are automatically calculated.
How do I register in the network?
Use registerAndActivate() with a referrer address. Dynamic registration price based on total wallets.
Can I register for free?
Yes, use registerFree() by burning tokens equivalent to 23 DAI value.
How do binary points work?
Maximum 2 points per purchase cycle with 4-week cooldown. Maximum 100 points per user. Initial points: 5 for paid registration, 0 for free registration.
What are monthly rewards?
3% of deposited amount per month for users with at least one empty position. Maximum up to 100% of deposited amount. Previous cashback is repaid first.
Can I transfer index ownership?
Yes, use the transferIndexOwnership() function to transfer index to a new address. All user data is transferred including points and history.
How do I purchase from vendors?
Use the purchase() function with amount, payout percentage (30-100%), and seller address. 5% tokens are burned, 50% goes to binary pool, 50% to vendor.
How does the voting system work?
Use voteUser() to like or dislike any registered user. You can change your vote anytime. Total likes and dislikes are publicly visible.
