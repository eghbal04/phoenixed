📊 Subscription and Point System

The IAM token has a subscription mechanism. The distribution of the subscription fee is as follows:

  • 10% for referral rewards (inviter)
  • 10% for token burning, which increases the token's value
  • 80% is distributed among users based on the points they earn

Each person can earn up to 5 points every 12 hours, with a maximum of 10 points per day. Any extra points overflow onto other points, increasing the value of each point.

💰 Subscription Refund Mechanism

People who are unable to earn points need not worry. The full amount they pay for a subscription will be refunded to them in the form of tokens. This refund occurs monthly at a rate of 3% of the amount paid until the full subscription fee is returned.

🎯 Activities and Rewards with the Token

All activities, including receiving rewards and commissions, are carried out with the IAM token. Each time a buy or sell transaction is made, the user pays a 2% fee, which is deposited into the token's financial reserve:

  • Converting DAI to IAM (buy) = token creation
  • Converting IAM to DAI (sell) = tokens are burned

All these activities result in token burning and increase the token's value. The system also functions as a binary network, where points and rewards are distributed transparently and fairly among users.

📈 Binary Points Overview

Binary points are governed by the smart contract and accrue based on eligibility within the network structure. Exact limits, intervals, and distributions are enforced on-chain and may evolve with contract parameters.

🔥 Deflationary Ecosystem

This ecosystem experiences severe negative inflation (deflation). The token growth logic is fundamentally different from traditional systems - instead of creating more tokens, the system continuously removes tokens from circulation, creating a deflationary pressure that drives price appreciation.

🔥 Token Burning Mechanisms

In every major activity within the ecosystem, a portion of tokens is permanently burned (removed from circulation). This creates a continuous reduction in total supply, leading to increased scarcity and value appreciation.

Token supply dynamics (including any burn mechanisms) are enforced directly by the current smart contract parameters and may differ from earlier versions. Please refer to live on-chain metrics for the latest behavior.

📈 Deflationary Impact

Every transaction removes tokens from circulation. This continuous reduction in total supply creates a deflationary spiral where:

  • Scarcity increases with every transaction
  • Token value appreciates due to reduced supply
  • Upward price pressure is maintained continuously
  • Ecosystem balance is preserved through controlled deflation

This mechanism causes severe price increases as the token becomes increasingly scarce. Unlike inflationary systems that dilute value, this deflationary model ensures that every token holder benefits from the continuous reduction in supply.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy IAM tokens?
Use the buyTokens() function with DAI. Minimum 1 DAI required. Dynamic buy cap based on contract balance.
How do I sell IAM tokens?
Use the sellTokens() function. Minimum 1 IAM required. No sell cap restriction.
What are the fees?
0% developer fee + dynamic backing fee (1-2% based on contract balance). Fees are automatically calculated.
How do I register in the network?
Use registerAndActivate() with a referrer address. Dynamic registration price based on total wallets.
Can I register for free?
Yes, use registerFree() by burning tokens equivalent to 23 DAI value.
How do binary points work?
Maximum 2 points per purchase cycle with 4-week cooldown. Maximum 100 points per user. Initial points: 5 for paid registration, 0 for free registration.
What are monthly rewards?
3% of deposited amount per month for users with at least one empty position. Maximum up to 100% of deposited amount. Previous cashback is repaid first.
Can I transfer index ownership?
Yes, use the transferIndexOwnership() function to transfer index to a new address. All user data is transferred including points and history.
How do I purchase from vendors?
Use the purchase() function with amount, payout percentage (30-100%), and seller address. 5% tokens are burned, 50% goes to binary pool, 50% to vendor.
How does the voting system work?
Use voteUser() to like or dislike any registered user. You can change your vote anytime. Total likes and dislikes are publicly visible.
